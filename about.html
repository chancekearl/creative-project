My name is Chance and this is a showcase of my work. I am currently a student at Brigham Young University. I haven't experienced a whole lot but I was able to live in England for two years and meet a ton of people from different backgrounds. I hope to be able to reach out to everyone I have ever met and meet even more people throughout my career.
